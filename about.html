About

Rifqi Abdillah is a Lecturer and Researcher in Informatics Engineering with a strong interdisciplinary background in Artificial Intelligence, Internet of Things (IoT), and Electronic Engineering. He holds a Master’s Degree in Informatics Engineering from Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember (ITS) with a GPA of 3.93/4.00 and a Bachelor’s Degree in Electronic Engineering from the Electronic Engineering Polytechnic Institute of Surabaya. His academic and research interests include Biomedical Informatics, Edge Computing, Image Processing, Machine Learning, and intelligent IoT systems, with a particular focus on non-invasive biomedical signal processing and data-driven decision-making.

Professional Profile

He is currently serving as a Lecturer at Universitas Negeri Surabaya (UNESA), where he is actively involved in teaching, curriculum development, and research activities in computer science and informatics technology. In addition to his academic role, he has extensive experience as an AIoT Engineer, Data Analyst, and Robotics Trainer, contributing to both academic research and industry-oriented innovation. He has authored multiple scientific publications, holds several granted patents in non-invasive health monitoring technologies, and has received national and international awards for his contributions to AI and IoT innovation. His professional mission is to advance impactful research while fostering practical, project-based learning to prepare future generations for challenges in intelligent and connected systems.
